The Hidden Costs of Shopify

What you see isn't always what you pay. Shopify's "low" entry price masks a mountain of recurring expenses.

App Store Trap

Basic features like product filters, WhatsApp buttons, or bulk discounts on Shopify require paid apps.

Typical Cost: +$50/month

Success Penalty

Shopify charges you a fee every time you make a sale. The more successful you become, the more they take.

Impact: 2% of total revenue

Theme Limitation

Professional, high-converting themes on Shopify often cost a significant one-time fee or monthly lease.

Theme Cost: $180 - $350
