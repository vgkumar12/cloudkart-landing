Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets through our secure payment gateway.

Is there a setup fee?

All cloud-hosted plans include free setup. If you wish to host the platform on your own server, a one-time setup fee of ₹3,000 applies.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Your data remains accessible for 30 days.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. Contact our sales team for custom pricing on multi-year contracts.

What about data security?

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We use SSL certificates, regular backups, and comply with industry security standards.
